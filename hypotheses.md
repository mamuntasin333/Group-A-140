\## Null and Alternative Hypotheses



\### Null Hypothesis (H0)



There is no difference in the mean house price between properties located within 10 km of Melbourne CBD and those located more than 10 km from the CBD.



---



\### Alternative Hypothesis (H1)



There is a difference in the mean house price between properties located within 10 km of Melbourne CBD and those located more than 10 km from the CBD.
